What this is

A genie built from nothing

No pretrained weights, no fine-tune on someone else's foundation model. TinyGenie is a GPT-style transformer trained from byte one — its own tokenizer, its own weights, all of it built and trained from scratch on a single laptop GPU. It's currently learning English and Chinese together, plus whatever the people reading this decide to teach it.

Why it matters

The internet's "average" text isn't how anyone actually talks

The base training corpus comes from large web scrapes — mostly news, reference writing, and formal prose in both languages. That gets a model fluent in textbook English and textbook Chinese. It does nothing for the way real conversations actually sound.

Submitted text gets folded into the corpus and oversampled 20× against everything else. A few hundred real lines from you outweighs entire scraped paragraphs of formal text.
